Jose Rizal
  • Full Name:José Protasio Rizal Mercado y Alonso Realonda
  • Life and Death Date: June 19, 1861-December 30 1896
  • Place of Birth: Calamba, Philippines
  • Educational Journey: University of Santo Tomas, Complutense University of Madrid, University of Santo Tomas Faculty of Medicine and Surgery, Heidelberg University, Ateneo de Manila University
  • Founded: La Liga Filipina
  • Jose Rizal is widely regarded as the Philippine national hero.
  • Rizal's novels"Noli me Tangere" and El Filibusterismo" exposed the injustices of Spanish rule and were influential in the Philippine reform movement.
  • He founded Liga Filipina, a nonviolent reform society, with the goal of uniting Filipinos in their fight for change.

Apolinario Mabini
  • Full Name:Apolinario Mabini y Maranan
  • Life and Death Date: July 23, 1864-May 13,1903
  • Place of Birth: Barrio Talaga, Tanauan, Batangas
  • Educational Journey: Colegio de San Juan de Letran, University of Santo Tomas
  • Despite being paralyzed by polio, Mabini became a legal and constitutional adviser during the Philippine Revolution.
  • His intellectual works, such as “El Verdadero Decálogo” and “Programa Constitucional de la República Filipina,” were instrumental in crafting the Malolos Constitution.
  • Mabini faced challenges during the Philippine-American War and was captured and exiled to Guam by American authorities.
Marcelo H. Del Pilar
  • Full Name:Marcelo Hilario del Pilar y Gatmaitán
  • Life and Death Date: August 30, 1850-July 4,1896
  • Place of Birth: Bulakan, Bulacan
  • Educational Journey: Colegio de San Juan de Letran,Colegio de San José,University of Santo Tomas
  • Del Pilar’s most notable work was his involvement in Dyaryong Tagalog, the first bilingual newspaper in the Philippines.
  • Del Pilar’s ability to connect with the masses through his writings made him a key figure in the nationalist movement.
  • Del Pilar’s contributions to the Propaganda Movement and his influence on the revolutionaries earned him a place as one of the most respected figures in Philippine history..
Emilio Aguinaldo
  • Full Name:Emilio Aguinaldo y Famy
  • Life and Death Date: March 22, 1869- February 6, 1964
  • Place of Birth: Kawit, Philippines
  • Educational Journey: Colegio de San Juan de Letran
  • Founded: Universidad Literaria y Cientifica de Filipinas, Association of Veterans of the Revolution
  • Leader of the Philippine forces against Spain during the Philippine Revolution and the Spanish–American War.
  • Military leader against the United States during the Philippine–American War.
  • President of the Revolutionary Government from June 1898 and January 1899.
